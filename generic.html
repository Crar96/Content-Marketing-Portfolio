About me

I am an inquisitive and proactive marketing professional with a keen eye for detail and a passion for problem-solving and performance measurement. I am creatively driven with an analytical mindset and always keen to refine my skillset and discover new ways to drive engagement and revenue through engaging digital content.

My experience as a Content Marketing Manager has allowed me to become highly proficient in the creation of original content, including E-books, landing pages, white papers, blogs, and executive reports. My skills go beyond just copywriting; after becoming inspired by the efforts of software developers while working for Ogury, I made it my duty to upskill and add more technical abilities to my repertoire.

I am particularly interested in marketing attribution and measuring how thought leadership reports and targeted marketing collateral can drive incremental revenue and open new sales pipeline. In November 2023, I also began a bootcamp in Front-end development and put those skills to use in the creation and hosting of this website.

Some of my work duties have included:

  • Researching and writing articles, blogs, E-books, executive reports, and white papers
  • Collaborating with graphic designers and free lancers to develop outstanding digital content and corporate assets
  • Principal copywriting duties for case studies, sales collateral, landing pages and one-pagers
  • Developing and iterating on product messaging architecture
  • Managing newsletters
  • Marketing automation duties via Adobe Marketo
  • CRM database management via Salesforce
  • Collaborating with PR and communications to execute GTM and content marketing strategies in press and publisher networks
  • Social media management and scheduling via Sprout Social
